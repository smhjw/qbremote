## qbitremote 0.1.7

- Added Android 15 edge-to-edge compatibility for smoother system bar behavior on modern devices.
- Refined the qbitremote branding with a cleaner, unified wordmark.
- Simplified custom themes: choose an image and apply it directly with the system photo picker.
- Improved custom background quality with original-file saving and screen-sized async decoding.
- Made dashboard startup feel faster with parallel loading, cached snapshots, and skeleton placeholders.
- Smoothed out long-press card reordering and reduced drag jank on the home screen.
